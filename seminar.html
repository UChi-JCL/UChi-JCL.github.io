AI+System Seminar

University of Chicago

About

Welcome to the AI + System Seminar! The seminar is broadly initiated by faculty and students interested in the frontier of AI and systems. It aims to bring together students and faculty at UChicago and external speakers to share their latest research in related areas and inspire research to address the challenges arising in various AI ecosystems.

The AI + System Seminar is open to anyone broadly interested in AI and systems.

Contact: Sanjay Krishnan, Raul Castro Fernandez, Junchen Jiang
